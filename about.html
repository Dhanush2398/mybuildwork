FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is My Build Work?

My Build Work is a comprehensive construction CRM that helps you manage workers, projects, stock, and client updates — all in one place.

How does punch-in with GPS and selfie work?

Workers punch in/out and take breaks by submitting a selfie along with GPS location, ensuring accurate attendance tracking at project sites.

Can I track project-wise stock usage?

Yes, the system allows real-time tracking of stock usage and remaining stock for each project individually, including image-based transfer records.

Is there a client login to view progress?

Clients get their own login to view daily work progress, used materials, stock details, and photos uploaded by site supervisors.

How are stock transfers handled?

Stock transfers are recorded with the quantity, project name, vehicle number, location, and image, making it transparent and traceable.

Can I download reports of work done?

Yes, you can generate daily, weekly, or monthly reports for attendance, work hours, stock usage, and project performance.

How secure is My Build Work?

We use industry-standard encryption and secure cloud infrastructure to keep your data safe and accessible only to authorized users.

Is training provided for staff?

Yes, we offer onboarding sessions, video tutorials, and support to help your team get started and make the most of My Build Work.
